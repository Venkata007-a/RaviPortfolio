My Projects & Impact

Supply Chain Optimization Through Data Analytics

Lead Data Analyst & Process Architect

Faced with escalating logistics costs and inefficiencies, a manufacturing client sought a comprehensive solution to streamline their supply chain operations.

  • Designed and implemented a **data collection framework** integrating disparate data sources (ERP, WMS, CRM).
  • Developed **SQL queries and Python scripts** to clean, transform, and load large datasets for analysis.
  • Built **interactive Power BI dashboards** visualizing key metrics like lead times, inventory turns, and supplier performance.
  • Identified bottlenecks in warehousing and transportation through **process mapping and bottleneck analysis**.
  • Automated routine data reconciliation tasks using **VBA and Power Query**, reducing manual effort by 70%.
  • Provided actionable insights that led to renegotiated supplier contracts and optimized delivery routes.

**Tools:** SQL Server, Python, Power BI, Excel (VBA/Power Query), Visio

Key Results:

  • **15% reduction in overall logistics costs** within 12 months.
  • **20% improvement in on-time delivery rates**.
  • **Reduced data processing time by 80%**, freeing up team resources.
  • Enhanced visibility into supply chain performance, enabling proactive decision-making.

Automated Financial Reporting System for a Mid-Sized Firm

Process Automation Specialist

A financial services firm was struggling with manual, error-prone monthly and quarterly report generation, leading to delays and compliance risks.

  • Conducted a detailed **process analysis** of existing reporting procedures, identifying key pain points and automation opportunities.
  • Developed a robust **RPA solution using UiPath** to automate data extraction from multiple legacy systems.
  • Designed **Excel-based templates with complex VBA macros** for automated data consolidation and report formatting.
  • Implemented **data validation rules** within the automation to ensure accuracy and reduce human error.
  • Created comprehensive documentation and provided training for end-users on the new automated system.
  • Integrated the solution with email for automated distribution of final reports to stakeholders.

**Tools:** UiPath, Microsoft Excel (VBA), SQL, SharePoint

Key Results:

  • **90% reduction in time spent on monthly financial report generation.**
  • **Eliminated 100% of data entry errors** in the reporting process.
  • **Saved an estimated 40 man-hours per month**, allowing staff to focus on analysis rather than data preparation.
  • Improved report accuracy and compliance adherence.
